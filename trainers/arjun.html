THE STORY

Arjun Krishnan grew up watching his father lift in a small garage gym in Thrissur. By the time he was 18, he was squatting double his bodyweight. His obsession with raw strength led him to compete in his first powerlifting meet at 20 — and he hasn't stopped since.

Over a decade of competing at district and state level, Arjun accumulated three district championship titles and a reputation as one of the most technically sound powerlifters in Kerala. Coaches travelled from neighbouring districts just to observe his coaching sessions.

At Reborn Fitness Studio, Arjun oversees all strength and powerlifting programming. His philosophy is rooted in precision — perfect form before heavy weight, every single time. Clients who train under Arjun don't just get stronger, they get smarter about strength.
