THE STORY

Vishnu Pillai ran the 100m for Kerala at the Junior National Athletics Championship in 2014. That early taste of elite sport shaped every aspect of his coaching career — the discipline, the attention to recovery, and the relentless pursuit of the 1% improvements that separate good from great.

After retiring from competitive sprinting at 24 due to a hamstring injury, Vishnu channelled his athletic background into performance coaching. He trained under a former Indian national athletics coach in Kozhikode for two years before joining Reborn Fitness Studio as its athletic performance specialist.

Vishnu works primarily with athletes, young sports enthusiasts, and gym members looking to improve explosive power, agility, and cardiovascular conditioning. His sprint and plyometric sessions are brutally effective — and brutally popular. If you want to move better, Vishnu is your coach.
