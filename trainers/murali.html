THE STORY

Murali Krishna is built differently — and not just physically. When you see him on stage, oiled and shredded to the bone, what you're seeing is the result of over a decade of relentless, uncompromising dedication to the craft of bodybuilding. He didn't just train hard. He trained smart, and he trained consistently.

Growing up in Kerala, Murali was drawn to the iron early. By his mid-twenties he was already a formidable presence on the regional competition circuit, earning titles across Thrissur district and attracting attention from coaches across the state. His conditioning — particularly his razor-sharp definition and stage presence — became his trademark.

As a coach at Reborn Fitness Studio, Murali brings the same intensity he carries on stage into every session he delivers. He specialises in competition prep, helping athletes peak at exactly the right moment. But he's just as passionate about transforming everyday members — because every great physique, he believes, starts with the same decision to begin.
