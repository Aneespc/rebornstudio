THE STORY

Rahul Menon's transformation story is one of Reborn's most powerful. At 23, he weighed 112kg and was pre-diabetic. Within 18 months, through nutrition science and structured training, he lost 38kg and reversed his diagnosis entirely. He then dedicated his life to helping others do the same.

Rahul holds a diploma in sports nutrition from the Sports Authority of India and completed his personal training certification with distinction. His approach is methodical and evidence-based — no fad diets, no unsustainable protocols, no shortcuts that don't hold up six months later.

He has coached over 220 clients through structured fat loss programmes, with an average client losing 12kg in their first 12 weeks. More importantly, the majority of his clients have maintained their results long-term — a statistic Rahul considers his greatest professional achievement.
