THE STORY

Thamjid Thachu didn't walk into a gym — he was forged in one. Growing up in Guruvayoor, he discovered the iron at the age of 17 and never looked back. What started as a passion quickly became a calling, and within three years he was competing at the district level.

By 2019, Thamjid stood on the Kerala State Bodybuilding Championship stage and walked away with the gold. His conditioning, symmetry, and stage presence turned heads across the region — earning him the title of one of Kerala's most promising young bodybuilders.

Today, as Head Coach at Reborn Fitness Studio, Thamjid channels everything he learned on stage into his clients' journeys. His coaching philosophy is simple: discipline over motivation, consistency over intensity, and identity over goals.
