THE STORY

Devika Nair came to fitness through injury. A former classical dancer who suffered a serious knee injury at 22, she turned to yoga and functional movement for rehabilitation — and discovered a passion that would define the rest of her life.

After earning her certification from the Indian Yoga Association and completing advanced functional movement training in Bangalore, Devika returned to Guruvayoor determined to bring a different dimension to local fitness culture. One that prioritised longevity, not just aesthetics.

Her classes at Reborn are often described as the hardest easy thing you'll ever do. Breathwork, mobility flows, and functional patterns that expose the weaknesses that heavy lifting hides. Many of Reborn's bodybuilding athletes credit Devika's sessions for keeping them injury-free through competition prep.
